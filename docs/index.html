You Are Welcome Here

Come As You Are

At Mercies Cross Ministries, everyone is welcome at the table. Whether you're a lifelong believer or just beginning to explore your faith, our doors are open and our community is here to walk alongside you. We are a congregation rooted in Scripture, fueled by love, and dedicated to serving our neighbors in Ironton and beyond.
